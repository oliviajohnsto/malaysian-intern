ABOUT

Intern For Good is a database of internships in non-profits, think tanks, government agencies and civil society organisations in Malaysia. Intern For Good was created in response to the fragmented communities of passionate nation builders. No organisation nor movement currently makes an effort to bring together interns from various do-good sectors. By fostering intergroup knowledge sharing, we hope to become a catalyst for collaboration and greater understanding.
